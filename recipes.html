I really love food with a passion but my two favorite foods are Buffalo Chicken Pizza and Mac and Cheese. Included are my favorite recipes.

Buffalo Chicken Pizza

  1. Preheat the oven to 400.
  2. Dice the cooked chicken.
  3. Melt the butter, add the hot sauce and toss with the chicken.
  4. Spread the ranch dressing on the pizza crust.
  5. Top with the chicken.
  6. Sprinkle on the cheese and bake for 10-12 minutes (or until the cheese is lightly browned).

Mac and Cheese

  1. Cook macaroni according to box directions and set aside.
  2. In a medium saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add flour and stir constantly until smooth.
  3. Turn heat to medium and add milk stirring constantly until thick. (About 10 minutes).
  4. Add hot sauce. Stir until mixed in evenly.
  5. Add pasta, bacon and cheese. Mix until cheese melts, bacon is mixed in, and pasta is evenly coated.
  6. Pour macaroni and cheese into a 9x13 baking dish and top according to taste.
  7. Bake in preheated oven at 350 degrees for about 25-30 minutes. (If using parmesan cheese and breadcrumbs take it out when the top looks lightly browned. If using cheddar cheese topping take out when top becomes darkened, but not burnt.).
